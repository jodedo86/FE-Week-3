Famous Attractions

The most serene and picturesque areas of Washington State. Make sure to take lots of pictures!

Mount Rainier

Are you ready to visit the tallest mountain in the state of Washington? This active volcano, currently at rest, last erupted in 1894. There are many activities for kids and families at Mount Rainier Park. As well, as breathtaking views to experience.

Orcas Island

Several whale watching tours take place on this charming island. If you've always wanted to try sailing you can book a three hour sailing excursion where you learn all about sailing including terminology and navigation.
